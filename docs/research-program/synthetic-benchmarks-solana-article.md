# Synthetic benchmarks to controlled Solana testbeds article

This article develops the methodological framing for synthetic benchmarks evolving into reproducible, observable, and control-ready experimental testbeds. It uses the containerised Solana localnet as a case study.

## Current status

- Article draft: v1.0 empirical pilot
- Primary location: `paper/synthetic-benchmarks-solana/`
- Empirical status: preliminary single-host pilot included
- Stronger claims still require: observability-overhead runs, Geyser/Yellowstone stream-latency runs, cross-host repetitions, and improved asynchronous workload generation.

## Claim boundary

The v1.0 pilot validates the measurement workflow and distinguishes baseline, low-load, and medium-load profiles. It does not establish public Solana mainnet performance, production cluster behaviour, or controller performance.
